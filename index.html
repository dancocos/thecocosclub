The Cocos Club

The Cocos Club, where Washingtonians come to relax and socialize. Featuring weekly events and guest musicians. Our curated wine, beer and cocktail lists are sure impress and don't miss our expansive whiskey and bourbon selection. Dine on our handcrafted recipes by our in house chef sourcing from local farms, creameries, bakeries and butchers. Enjoy the music selections of DJ Thos
